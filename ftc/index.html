WHO WE ARE

Gradle Demons and the Mavens of Mayhem are FIRST® TECH CHALLENGE TEAMS 4054 & 4053 based in Tacoma, WA. Both of us are here to have fun and embody the FIRST® ethos, Gracious Professionalism®.

Both the Gradle Demons and the Mavens of Mayhem are sister teams that stem down from the FRC Team 2557 - The SOTAbots, where it is used as a stepping stone for anyone who is interested in robotics.
